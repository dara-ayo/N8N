# Platform Formatting Checklists (from Fetemi Google Doc)

## LinkedIn Post

Checklist:
- Use the PAS (Problem -- Agitate -- Solution) copywriting technique
- Short paragraphs (max 2-3 lines)
- Bullets or symbols (checkmarks, dots) for clarity
- 3-5 relevant emojis max
- End with a clear CTA
- Use a relevant image or carousel if possible

Example:
> Ever wondered why your morning meeting drains your energy?
>
> Try this:
> - Block mornings for deep work
> - Skip email until after coffee
> - End your day with next-day planning
>
> Your productivity will thank you.
>
> What's one ritual you swear by?

## Tweet (X)

Checklist:
- Lead with the main benefit or insight
- One core idea per tweet
- Optional line breaks for readability
- Max 1-2 hashtags, keep them relevant
- Tag only if it adds value

Example:
> Boost your focus in under 5 minutes.
>
> Do a "brain dump": write down everything on your mind, then circle your top 3 tasks.
>
> Feels like clearing browser tabs in your head.
>
> #ProductivityTips

## Email Newsletter

Checklist:
- Strong subject line (clear benefit or intrigue).
- Short, engaging intro (1-3 sentences).
- Main value section (story, tips, or resource) -- make it skimmable with subheadings or bullets.
- Optional secondary item (quick extra tip, link, or update).
- Clear call-to-action (read, download, reply, share).
- Friendly sign-off (consistent signature or tagline).
- Tone and style rule of thumb: Imagine you're writing to a smart friend who's busy but trusts you to send something worth their time.
- 250-600 words
