# SEO Best Practices (from Fetemi Google Doc)

1. **Keyword Integration**
   - Get primary keywords from the content idea, integrate it into the title of the article and in the first 100 words of the article.
   - Analyze top-performing articles to get long-tail keywords and short-tail keywords. Then integrate these keywords into the body and headers of the articles.

2. **Structure Optimization**
   - One H1 title → H2 section headers → H3 subheaders
   - 700-800 words per main section (informed by top articles)
   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)

3. **Content Enrichment**
   - 2-3 relevant internal or external links.
   - Readability at Grade 7 level
   - Include 1 contextually relevant image
